Previously, Robotics Research & LLM Post-training @ Preferred Networks, Inc

I graduated with a Masters in Informatics at Università della Svizzera italiana supervised by Prof. Jürgen Schmidhuber and Dr. Paulo E. Rauber

My research interests center around Robotic Manipulation in the presence of Uncertainty, Deep Reinforcement Learning (DRL) and Implicit Representations.
